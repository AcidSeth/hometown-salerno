Minerva Garden

Il Giardino della Minerva, a hidden gem nestled in the hills of Salerno, once served as a vital source of medicinal plants for the esteemed Medical School of Salerno. Among its diverse flora, you'll find ancient remedies like the mystical mandrake, once believed to hold extraordinary powers.

Despite their modest size, these gardens offer a serene retreat, perfect for escaping the midday heat. Shaded pergolas and a sophisticated irrigation system—complete with fountains, streams, and natural springs—create a refreshing atmosphere. As you wander through its five terraced levels, the panoramic views of Salerno's old town and harbor unfold before you, each vista more breathtaking than the last.

Before you leave, pause to enjoy the garden’s harvest in a charming tearoom with outdoor seating, where the flavors of the plants grown within reach your plate.

Cozy alleyways

The alleyways of Salerno are narrow, cobblestone paths that wind through the city’s historic heart. Lined with old stone buildings and filled with small shops and cafes that offer a glimpse into daily life.

Despite their age, these alleys are still vibrant, providing hidden courtyards, local treasures, and occasional views of the sea.
